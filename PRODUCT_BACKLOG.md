# SmartBacklog - Product Backlog

## Project Overview
**Title:** SmartBacklog - The intelligent assistant for Agile product management  
**Objective:** Web-based task management application (Kanban style) that assists Product Owners and development teams by using AI to automate tedious parts of project management.
